Prototype notes — v1.2

  • Company display line: Shows "Cableway Development Company" — no "CDC" prefix because the badge logo already represents CDC visually. Legal disclaimer uses the full name "CDC Cableway Development Company and its affiliates".
  • Icons: SVG files used via <img src> for visual prototype review. Production email requires PNG export at 56×56 px (2×). Future step
  • Image hosting: Relative paths for local review only. Production requires permanent public HTTPS URLs on company-controlled hosting. Future step
  • Brand orange: Working value #F16623. Confirm exact HEX from brand team before production.
  • Column widths: Logo 90 px · spacer 22 px · identity 258 px · separator 1 px · spacer 21 px · award 248 px = 640 px total. Logo at 90 px (reduced from 96 px after cropped asset replacement). Award widened from 240 → 248 px; logo-spacer trimmed 30 → 22 px to maintain 640 px total.
  • Gmail compatibility: All CSS is inline. No <style> blocks, no web fonts, no base64 images, no inline SVG elements.
  • Separators: 1 px bgcolor td — more reliable than CSS border in email clients.
  • Award banner: Written approval of FT/Statista logo use in email signatures required before production.
