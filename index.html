How to install on iPhone

  1. Click Copy Apple Mail / iPhone Signature above.
  2. On your iPhone, open Settings → Mail → Signature.
  3. Tap into the signature field and select all existing text.
  4. Paste (long-press → Paste). The formatted signature appears.
  5. Tap Mail (top left) to save.

Copy must be done on a Mac or PC. iPhone clipboard does not preserve formatting.

After pasting into iPhone Mail Signature settings, create a new email to verify that the signature appears on a white background.

Note: On iPhone, especially in Dark Mode, the signature may look different while editing in Settings or composing a message. Always send a test email and check the received message — this is the final rendering.
